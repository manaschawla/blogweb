About My Blogs

My Blogs is a growing Indian Blogs brand offering curated blogs across diverse categories like tech, fashion, and essentials. We are committed to making online blogs simpler, faster, and more reachable for everyone.

Since our inception in 2025, we have focused on quality, affordability, and uploading blogs. What began as a simple idea has evolved into a full-fledged online platform trusted by thousands. Our goal is to deliver happiness and knowlegable blogs at a time.

Meet Our Founder

Manas Chawla, the founder of My Blogs, is a passionate entrepreneur with a clear mission—to revolutionize online blogs-reading in India. With a background in technology and business, Manas brings both innovation and empathy to the table.

His vision has shaped the company's strong values: customer satisfaction, honesty, and ease of access. Under his leadership, the company continues to grow with a focus on trust and service excellence.
